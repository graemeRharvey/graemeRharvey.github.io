About Me

A wearer of many hats (figuratively only...you'll almost never catch me wearing one), a Tester turned Programmer turned Leader, and really just an all around enabler. I now use what I've learned over the years to help teams perform at their best.


Bored of talking about work? Talk to me about:
  • Parenthood: I have a young child and I could talk about him forever.
  • Animals: I have a dog (Dante), and two cats (Champ and Minnie).
  • Sports: I play Ultimate and enjoy following the Raptors.
  • Automotive: I grew up around old British sports cars, and have driven in multiple types of motorsports over the years.
